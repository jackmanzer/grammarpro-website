About

A homeschooled child of a former college professor and a school teacher, Jack had a rather unique upbringing. A love of learning was fostered from the start, along with an emphasis on the written word and the intellectual life.

Currently, Jack wears multiple hats: aside from tutoring, he also does freelance web and instructional design. But tutoring is by far his favorite hat, for the the little victories and “light bulb moments” that make the occupation so rewarding.
