College Writing Prep

While working in the campus writing center at Southwestern University, I found that most students were ill-prepared for the rigors of college level writing. These sessions are designed to develop post-secondary writing competency by building skills in two key areas:

Composition

  • how to construct clear, logical arguments through analyzing essays written by respected authors.
  • how to maximize the writing process to improve creative thinking and thesis generation.
  • how to find an extract evidence/points from good sources.
  • how to create content using the writers toolbox.
  • how to build an academic vocabulary.

Grammar

  • twelve lessons cover all necessary topics to obtain the clear understanding of grammatical correct writing necessary to succeed in college.

Scheduling An Appointment

Sessions may be sheduled at the desired frequency, though once per week is recommended. Tutoring is considered complete when the student is able to write a clear, coherent, well-researched essay about a post-secondary text or topic without assistance.
