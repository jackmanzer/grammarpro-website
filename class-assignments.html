Class Assignments

I specialize in assisting with humanities’ essays and term papers for high school and college-level courses, though I have aided students with more extensive assignments (senior theses, master theses, dissertations etc.). I can help you at any stage in the writing process:

  • Brainstorming and thesis development
  • Finding and extract evidence and points from good sources
  • Textual analysis
  • Organization and flow
  • Grammar errors
  • Style and tone
  • Citation questions

If you would like ongoing help with a class, you can schedule a periodic appointment. If you just need assistance with a particular assignment, you’re welcome to schedule one or more sessions as needed.
